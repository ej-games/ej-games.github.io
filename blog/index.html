7/4/22 - Controversial Colours Cleared

It's been a long time since my idea of adding colour schemes was considered.
And I've realised: colours are, apparently, a very touchy subject. Take the Aqua theme for example. It looked like this:



And it was completely 50/50 to whether people liked it or hated it.

Some people preferred the Dark theme. Some people preferred the one that was just grey with some blue.
I wanted to add colour schemes to try and minimalise this. To stop people from hating all of the colours.

Apparently, just the idea of adding another colour is enough to make people dislike it. Even if they don't have to pick it, they will still not like it.

I'm not angry at anyone. Or myself for the idea. It's just...
Why are opinions so... good at being expressed? It's all the opinion's fault. I'm not friends with Opinion any more.
