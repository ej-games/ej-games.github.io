Cookie Jar

Welcome to the main page of Cookie Jar, a simple, lightweight library for vanilla JavaScript.

Cookie Jar allows you to save data to LocalStorage or the document's cookie, with just a single function, and not much knowledge of how LocalStorage or cookies work.

Installing

Importing online
To import Cookie Jar into your HTML file, just add a script tag and set the src to the Cookie Jar URL:

<script src='https://vectarray.com/library/cookiejar/main.js'></script>

Downloading
Just click here and drag it into your website's root folder.

Then, add the following to your <head> section: 

<script src='/main.js'></script>
