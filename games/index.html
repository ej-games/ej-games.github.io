Word Game

Still under development. Contains many, many bugs, but you can still play it and give us feedback. Disclaimer: only verbal feedback is supported.

In the hit new "Word Game", you are given a few words with 2-4 missing letters. Your job is to guess the letters that are missing from all of them.
E.g.

F--H
EP--ODE
R--E

The answer is "IS" (FISH, EPISODE, RISE)
