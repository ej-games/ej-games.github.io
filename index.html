Currently Supported

Locked In

This is a top-down shooter that I'm currently working on. I started it in November(ish) 2021, and it's still going (as of March 2022)! In Locked In, you collect coins, fight enemies, kill bosses, upgrade your weapons, explore new zones, do other things, and some other things, and complete quests (soon to be added).

I'm making a new Roblox game? Yes, and it's going to be quite bad, seeing as I haven't even touched Roblox for a few months. It's going to be a simple parkour game, like Practise Basic Obby Skills, but a bit more simple, and not called Practise Basic Obby Skills.

ConnectCircle

This is a mobile game I made (surpise: using Unity) where you tap different coloured circles to get points. I even added reward ads to the game, and could get $0.10! But I need to get at least $100 to redeem it. It has speed upgrades, money upgrades, and even prestiging. And it's available for Android!

ConnectMore

This is another mobile game, but this time, you are dragging around a box to form patterns. You get points for forming different patterns, with more complex patterns rewarding more points. It has skins, a pattern viewer and, for some reason, a button to reset your data. It's also available for Android!

Obby Showdown

This is a roblox game I made! You have a controller, who places stages of parkour for the others to complete. If everyone dies, the controller wins. If the time runs out, the remaining runners win. I spent August 2021 making this amazing game! I didn't know, at the time, that it would be the last Roblox game I ever made, for now, at least.

This game is no longer updated.
