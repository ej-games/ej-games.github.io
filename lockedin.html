Locked In

Locked In is a top-down shooter, where you attack enemies with a wide range of guns.

SMGs!
Assault rifles!
Shotguns!
Knives!
What do you do in Locked In, apart from shoot things?

At the time of writing, I'm currently adding a story to the game. It involves John Gold, the only NPC (currently) and possibly another NPC being added soon.
While I'm making the story, I will try to update the game, but the updates will be a lot smaller, and NPCs will be removed until the story is complete.
