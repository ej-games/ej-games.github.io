Locked In is a top-down shooter, where you attack enemies with a wide range of guns.

It's the project that I'm devoting my current time to the most. Here's a summary of what the game is.

Guns

Locked In is a game where you mainly kill enemies. There is a wide range of guns to choose from, that have to be unlocked and bought with coins, that enemies drop.
There is a variety of different enemies, with more coming soon. There are zombies, robots, snowmen, thugs and many more.

Kill the enemies, take their coins, and upgrade your gear.

Bosses

What's a shooter game without bosses? To find a boss, just open a chest, and it has a chance of spawning a random boss. At the time of writing, there is only one boss, but the next update (V6) will have a new boss!
Bosses also drop coins, like all other enemies. They also sometimes drop exclusive weapons.#

Kill bosses, get their weapons, and defeat the waves of enemies that are coming.

Spells

The most special thing about Locked In is its spell and class feature. There are 5 classes, each with a spell and a passive.
They range from:
flaming walls of fire,
blinking through walls and magic arcane explosions.

Use your spells on the enemies to level the playing field. After all, there is a whole horde of them.

Zones

There are also different zones in the game! The further you search, the more diverse and unusual biomes you will find.
The darkness lies only a few hundred metres beyond the start... that's where the thugs stay...

Explore the zones, kill their enemies, and dive deeper into the dungeon.
