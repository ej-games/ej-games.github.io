Connect Circle


ConnectCircle was the third mobile game I ever developed, and it was the most mobile-game like one I've ever made.

It is only available for Android. To quickly download, click here.

Gameplay

This plays kind of like an idle game, or a clicker. But instead of just clicking one box, you click a certain colour. This then makes all of the circles on the screen of that colour, turn into a certain number of points.
You can then upgrade each colour, unlock new colours, and then "prestige" after unlocking all colours.

Also featured ads. Thank God I didn't upload it to the App Store, because I would probably be arrested for fraud. They never actually gave rewards.

Development

On a development high after ConnectMore, I thought "well, how much worse can I do" because my self-esteem is unmeasurably ... high.

What now?

Like ConnectMore, the future is not bright. The most likely outcome is that I will update it to work on the latest Android version, fix a few bugs, then sit as it slowly turns to app-slurry.
